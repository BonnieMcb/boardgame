café

Come and visit our café, where you can borrow boardgames from our extensive collection. If it's on our shop page, it's in our collection!

How it works:

  • You pay a cover fee to rent a table.
  • The charge is £4.50 for 2 hours.
  • Play as many games as you like.
  • Order drinks and snacks from our table service, so you don't need to waste valuable playing time queing for your drinks.
